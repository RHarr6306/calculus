Installation

  1. Make sure you have a calculator that is compatible with downloading programs. A TI-83 and above should be fine.
  2. Click on a link to download a program.
  3. If the program has a .py extension, you may run it from there by double clicking the program.
  4. If it is a calculator program, make sure your calculator is plugged in to your computer.
  5. Download the TI Connect CE Software and follow their steps to receive the program on your calculator.
